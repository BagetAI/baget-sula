The Sula Philosophy

Zero waste. Zero plastic. Complete circle.

Bespoke Ceramic Vessel

Each container is custom matte-glazed stoneware. Designed specifically with food-grade safety, heat-tolerant materials, and the perfect shape to turn into an espresso cup, planter, or stationery holder after burn-out.

100% Domestic Soy & Coconut

Clean, slow, soot-free burn sourced entirely from North American growers. Zero paraffin, toxic phthalates, or synthetic dyes, featuring natural wood wicks that crackle like a miniature hearth.

Compostable Shipping

Completely zero-plastic presentation. External packaging uses post-consumer waste (PCW) recycled paperboard and FSC-certified structural inserts, printed elegantly with water-based soy inks.
