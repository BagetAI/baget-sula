The Sula Formulation Protocol

Fourteen Days to Stability

Premium soy wax requires rigorous, temperature-controlled execution. Sula candles are heated to exactly 185°F, blended with customized phthalate-free botanical concentrates for two full minutes, and slowly poured at 135°F to ensure a flawlessly flat cream surface.

01
Pre-heated Ceramics Vessels are pre-warmed to 100°F prior to the pour. This completely eliminates "frosting" and ensures total container adhesion.
02
14-Day Optimal Cure Period Our candles sit in a dedicated humidity-controlled room for exactly two weeks to let the soy wax crystal structure fully lock with fragrance botanical molecules.
03
Patented Acoustic Crackle Using FSC-certified native softwood wicks licensed by Lumetique, Sula produces a comforting fireplace crackle with zero soot residue.
